Movement Guide

The Heart

  • When in the world, be with yourself in your heart. Not only is this the proper orientation for well-being, but also it will protect you.

The Muscles

  • Learn to use all of your muscles! Get them into the right positions.

The Mind

  • Commit the mind to move the body properly and in ways that are enjoyable from moment to moment.

The Goal

  • Be fluid, free, and happy. Do not concern yourself with appearances outside of protection.
